How to play:

Select the color peg you want to place and then put it in the current row. After you have a pattern of four pegs, take a guess! The game will place up to four pins next to your pattern. Each red pin means that you have a peg that is the right color and where it belongs. Each white pin means that you have a peg of a color that is in the pattern, but not in the right place. You have ten guesses to get the pattern right. Enjoy!
